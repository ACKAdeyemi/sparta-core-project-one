HOW TO PLAY

You are the PADDLE, your enemy is the BALL


Press 'A' Key to move LEFT

Press 'D' Key to move RIGHT

If ball hits top of screen = BALL +1 & POINTS +1

If ball hits bottom of screen = BALL -1

If all balls hit bottom of screen = GAME OVER


Only the best dare to beat the high score...
